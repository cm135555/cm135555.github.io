Mom's Quiche with Hashbrown Crust

This is a quiche my mom found online and it is AMAZING! It is a delicious combination of eggs, bacon, cheese, green onions, and more! It's REALLY good with hot sauce, too!

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 4-6
Ingredients Quantity
Refrigerated hashbrowns such as Simply Potatoes. 20 oz
Melted butter 1 tbsp
Eggs 9-12
Turkey bacon 6-8 cooked pieces
Shredded cheese (any blend) 3/4 cup
Half & Half 1/4 cup
Sliced green onion 1/4-1/2 cup
Salt & pepper for taste As much as you would like

Step 1: Preheat oven to 450°F.

Step 2: Combine melted butter and shredded hasbrowns into a casserole dish sprayed with cooking spray.

Step 3: Put into oven for 30-40 minutes.

Step 4: While butter and hasbrowns are cooking, combine remaining ingredients into a large mixing bowl.

Step 5: When hashbrown crust is done cooking, lower oven temperature to 350°F.

Step 6: Pour the egg mixture into the prepared hashbrown crust.

Step 7: Bake for about 30 minutes to desired doneness.

Step 8: Allow to slightly cool. Serve with sour cream, hot sauce, and/or extra green onion if desired. Enjoy!

Pepperoni Pizza (with store-bought dough and sauce)

This is a standard pepperoni pizza recipe I found online. It consists of pizza sauce, parmesan cheese, oregano, mozzarella cheese, and pepperoni.

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Tomato sauce 1 8oz can
Grated Parmesan cheese 1/4 cup
Dried oregano 1/4 teaspoon
Prebaked 12-inch thin pizza crust 1
Shredded part-skim mozzarella cheese 1 cup
Sliced pepperoni 1/3 cup

Step 1: Preheat oven to 450°.

Step 2: Combine parmesan cheese, oregano, and tomato sauce in a small bowl, and spread mixture evenly over the crust.

Step 3: Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese and finish with pepperoni.

Step 4: Place on ungreased pizza pan. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the edges of the crust and slightly browned and cheese is melted. Cut into 6 slices, serve, and enjoy!

My Sister's Taco Bites

These are some great taco bites made from 3 (6 if you add the optional stuff) simple ingredients, and they taste great, too! I call my sister Mini, so I like to think of these as "Mini (double meaning) taco bites."

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 12
Ingredients Quantity
Black beans 12 tablespoon
Corn 12 tablespoon
Large tortillas 3
(Optional) Shredded Cheese 12 teaspoons
(Optional) Pico De Gallo As much as you would like to add to each taco bite
(Optional) Jalapeno Slices 12

Step 1: Spray a 12-cup muffin tin generously with cooking spray.

Step 2: Cut the edges off your tortillas so you have 3 large squares. Cut each of the squares into 4 smaller squares, so you have 12 squares in total.

Step 3: Place 1 square into each cup in the muffin tin.

Step 4: Place 1 tablespoon of corn and black beans (and 1 teaspoon of cheese, if you want) into each cup.

Step 5: Bake for 10 minutes. Serve and enjoy!

(Optional:) Step 6: Top each cup with pico de gallo and a jalapeno slice before serving.
