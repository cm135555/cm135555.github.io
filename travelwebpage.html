Where?

Fiji is a tropical island located in the South Pacific Ocean, near Australia, New Zealand, and
Papua New Guinea.

Why?

Fiji is beautiful. It is known for its sandy beaches and coral reefs. It is also a nice place for
a getaway due to its location, which is likely not near you, and its warm weather and
climate.

When?

Overall, based on weather, crowds, pricing, etc., the best time to visit Fiji is from late October to early November, as prices are reasonable, there are less crowds, and the weather is pleasantly dry, warm, and sunny.

Where can you go?

When you go to Fiji, there are lots of places to go.

  • Bouma National Heritage park on Taveuni Island
  • "Adventure Capital of Fiji" Pacific Harbor on Viti Levu
  • Garden of the Sleeping Giant and Sabeto Hot Springs on Viti Levu

What can you see?

When you visit Fiji, you can see some cool things.

  • Sri Siva Subramaniya Swami Temple on Viti Levu
  • Mt. Tomanivi, an extinct volcano on Viti Levu
